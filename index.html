P&T Guitars is a cozy, family-run guitar and musical instrument shop in Hampshire. We've been in the business for over twenty years and have a wealth of experience in selling and servicing guitars, amplifiers and other musical instruments and accessories. As guitar players ourselves, we know how important finding the right instrument is. Our staff are happy to chat, make recommendations, and help you try the instruments and equipment. So next time you're in the area please feel free to pop in, say hello and hopefully find your dream six-stringed friend!
